6 ways to personalise any outfit

by Divya Sharma

27 Jun 2024
Photo credit: SbriStudio

From bespoke hats to beautifully crafted bags, these customised closet staples will put a fresh spin on your signature style. Photo credit: SbriStudio When it comes to building your wardrobe, the best rule you can follow is to not follow any rules. When your confidence shines because you feel good in what you put on, you automatically become a fashion icon. But one thing that can instantly elevate any look? A touch of personalisation. And while we love fun socks with our dog's face on them and hilarious pyjamas to celebrate the holidays with family, you can also put small and subtle personalisation touches on everything from jackets to purses for a wardrobe that feels both high-end and exactly like you. Talented sellers on Etsy dedicate themselves to creating fabulous finds, and these well-crafted pieces can be used for years to come – some might even become keepsakes in the future. Shop our personalised style guide now and get ready to transform your closet in 3, 2, 1…

Personalised jackets

Whether you're looking for a fun bridal jacket that you can rock long after the wedding is over or a fun denim jacket for everyday use, these custom jackets will make you wish it stayed chilly all year long just so you don't have to stop wearing them. Pair it with a summer dress when you know there will be a nip in the air by sundown or instantly transform your most comfortable jeans and tee when you throw it on – you’ll be turning heads either way.
